Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products and services

  • Do you offer bulk pricing for businesses?

    Yes, we offer competitive bulk pricing for businesses of all sizes. The discount depends on the quantity and product type. Please contact our sales team through the form above or call us directly to discuss your specific requirements and receive a customized quote.

  • What is your delivery timeframe?

    Our standard delivery timeframe is 3-5 business days within major cities and 5-7 business days for other areas. For urgent orders, we also offer express delivery options at an additional cost. International shipping is available with timeframes varying by destination.

  • Do you provide custom branded PPE solutions?

    Absolutely! We offer custom branding services for various PPE products including helmets, vests, and more. We can add your company logo, safety messages, or employee names. Minimum order quantities apply for custom items. Contact our team for detailed information about our customization options.

  • What safety standards do your products comply with?

    All our products comply with relevant South African National Standards (SANS) and international safety standards including ANSI, CE, EN, and ISO certifications where applicable. Each product listing includes specific compliance information, and we maintain documentation for all regulatory approvals.

  • Do you offer onsite safety assessments?

    Yes, our safety specialists can conduct comprehensive workplace safety assessments to identify potential hazards and recommend appropriate PPE solutions. This service is available for businesses within major metropolitan areas. Please contact us to schedule an assessment or learn more about our safety consulting services.

About Us

Discover the story behind Blannq's commitment to safety and excellence

South Africa's Trusted PPE Supplier

At Blannq, we have been at the forefront of providing high-quality personal protective equipment since our establishment. Our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure every worker returns home safely after a day's work.

With years of industry experience, we have built strong relationships with manufacturers and suppliers around the world, allowing us to offer a comprehensive range of safety equipment that meets international standards.

Our team of safety experts is dedicated to understanding your specific needs and providing tailored solutions that not only meet regulatory requirements but also enhance productivity and comfort in the workplace.

MOur Mission

Blannq is committed to enhancing workplace safety through the provision of high-quality personal protective equipment that meets international standards. We strive to be a reliable partner to businesses across South Africa by offering comprehensive safety solutions, exceptional customer service, and competitive pricing.

We aim to continuously educate our clients about the importance of proper safety equipment and practices, contributing to the reduction of workplace accidents and injuries.

VOur Vision

To be the leading provider of personal protective equipment in South Africa, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

We envision a future where every workplace prioritizes the safety and well-being of its employees, and Blannq is at the heart of this transformation, setting new standards in the PPE industry and expanding our positive impact across Africa.

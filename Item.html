Product Description

The Premium Safety Helmet X500 is engineered for professionals who demand the highest level of head protection in industrial environments. This premium-grade helmet exceeds all South African safety standards and has been rigorously tested to provide optimal protection against impacts, falling objects, and electrical hazards.

The X500 features a high-density polyethylene shell that provides superior impact resistance while maintaining a lightweight design for all-day comfort. The innovative 6-point suspension system distributes weight evenly across the head, reducing pressure points and fatigue during extended wear.

Designed with user comfort in mind, the X500 includes a ratchet adjustment system that allows for a quick and secure custom fit. The strategic ventilation openings provide excellent airflow, keeping workers cool even in the hottest conditions without compromising safety.

The helmet's shell is UV-stabilized to prevent degradation from sun exposure, ensuring a longer lifespan even in harsh outdoor environments. The X500 is also compatible with a wide range of accessories including face shields, earmuffs, and attachable lights, making it a versatile choice for various industries.

Blannq stands behind the quality of the X500 with a 2-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Choose the X500 for uncompromising safety and comfort that professionals trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace this safety helmet?

The X500 has a recommended service life of 5 years from date of first use, subject to regular inspections. However, if the helmet sustains an impact, shows signs of damage, or is exposed to harsh chemicals, it should be replaced immediately regardless of age.

Is this helmet suitable for electrical work?

Yes, the X500 meets Class E electrical requirements, providing protection against electrical hazards up to 1000V a.c. It's suitable for electrical work environments, but always follow proper safety protocols specific to your industry.

Can I attach accessories to this helmet?

Yes, the X500 features standard 30mm accessory slots that are compatible with most face shields, earmuffs, and attachable lamps. We recommend using Blannq accessories for guaranteed compatibility.

How should I clean this helmet?

Clean the helmet with mild soap and warm water. Avoid using solvents, abrasives, or harsh chemicals as these can damage the shell and reduce the helmet's protective properties. Allow to air dry naturally away from direct heat sources.

Product Overview

The Blannq Premium Safety Helmet X500 is designed to provide maximum head protection in demanding industrial environments. This premium hard hat features an innovative 6-point suspension system that distributes impact forces effectively, reducing the risk of head injuries during accidents.

The helmet shell is constructed with high-density, UV-resistant ABS plastic that's reinforced with strategic ridging to enhance structural integrity without adding unnecessary weight. At just 380 grams, the X500 provides all-day comfort without compromising on safety.

Comfort and Fit

We understand that safety equipment is only effective when it's worn consistently. That's why the X500 includes our patented ComfortFit‚Ñ¢ adjustment system, allowing for precise sizing between 52-64cm head circumferences. The ratchet mechanism enables easy one-handed adjustment, even while wearing gloves.

The moisture-wicking sweatband features antibacterial treatment to prevent odor buildup during extended use. Four ventilation channels promote airflow without compromising the helmet's electrical insulation properties, keeping workers cooler in hot conditions.

Versatility and Integration

The X500 helmet is designed as part of a comprehensive PPE system. Standard accessory slots are compatible with Blannq face shields, earmuffs, and headlamps (all sold separately). Reflective strips on all sides enhance visibility in low-light conditions, making this helmet ideal for roadwork, mining, and night shift operations.
